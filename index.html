Why Maptime?

Maptime is about:

  • Helping each other
  • Learning from and teaching together
  • Making a safe space for learning
  • Making maps more democratic
  • Bringing the Geo community together
  • Having fun!
Why Maptime?

Maptime is NOT about:

  • Judgement
  • Getting other people to do your work for free ←NO
  • Being too technical
  • Heckling
  • Sales pitches
  • Arguing
  • Violence
  • Having no fun :(

Maptime Boston: Let's celebrate mapping and geography in and of Boston!
